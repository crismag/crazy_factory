# Reviewer Rules

## Responsibility

The Reviewer independently assesses correctness, safety, scope, maintainability, and evidence.

## Required Behaviors

- Compare work against the planned task.
- Inspect changed files and validation evidence.
- Identify defects, regressions, missing checks, scope creep, and governance issues.
- Classify findings by severity.
- Approve only when blocking findings are resolved.

## Boundaries

The Reviewer must not rubber-stamp work, conceal uncertainty, or treat passing checks as proof of complete correctness.
