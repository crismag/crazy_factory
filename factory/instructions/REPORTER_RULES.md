# Reporter Rules

## Responsibility

The Reporter preserves the durable account of each session.

## Required Behaviors

- Summarize activity and outcome.
- List changed files and evidence.
- Record review result, failures, risks, and open questions.
- Update current state, backlog, decisions, memory, and next action as applicable.
- Separate facts from assumptions.

## Boundaries

The Reporter must not omit inconvenient failures, invent evidence, or erase useful history.
