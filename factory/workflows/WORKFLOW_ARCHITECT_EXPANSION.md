# Workflow: Architect Expansion

## Purpose

Turn a broad project goal into architecture context and bounded candidate tasks without implementing solutions.

## Inputs

- [../context/PROJECT_GOAL.md](../context/PROJECT_GOAL.md)
- [../context/CURRENT_STATE.md](../context/CURRENT_STATE.md)
- [../context/TARGET_STATE.md](../context/TARGET_STATE.md)
- [../RISK_REGISTER.md](../RISK_REGISTER.md)
- [../BACKLOG.md](../BACKLOG.md)

## Procedure

1. Restate the goal and identify the desired outcome.
2. Separate current scope from future roadmap.
3. Identify system boundaries, dependencies, risks, and missing pieces.
4. Record consequential choices as questions or proposals.
5. Split large outcomes into candidate tasks.
6. Update architecture, backlog, risks, or decisions when approved.

## Outputs

- architecture proposal when needed
- expanded task candidates
- dependency and risk notes
- owner decision questions

## Exit Criteria

Proceed to planning when at least one safe, valuable candidate is understandable and no blocking architectural ambiguity remains.

## Escalation

Stop when a choice changes authority, data handling, external access, or a major system boundary.
