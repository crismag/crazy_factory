# Known Risks

## Summary

The highest-priority risks are unsafe repository changes, scope creep, lost context, ungrounded local-model output, silent validation gaps, and unintended external data exposure.

## Operating Guidance

- Consult [../RISK_REGISTER.md](../RISK_REGISTER.md) before selecting consequential work.
- Add newly discovered risks promptly.
- Update mitigations when evidence changes.
- Escalate when a critical risk cannot be controlled within existing authority.
